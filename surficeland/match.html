City

If you're here to not only experience our world famous waves but to experience our architecture, nightlife and women, then the city is the best place for you. With hosts located all around our capital you'll find it hard not to wake up surrounded by an assortment of strange meats and Skallagrímur, our local mime .

Rural

Farms, farms and more farms. You might even get a glimpse of our mythical tractor. If you're not into the city life and want to stay nearer to the wilderness then our rural locations are perfect. with remote locations ranging from our hot springs to Hjörtþór's fish farm you'll never want to see other people again.

Coastal

If you're here to surf and only surf then do we have the best options for you. Scattered across the coast we have properties to suit the types who kind of like the inner city but can't stay too far from water otherwise they will have a mental breakdown, to the secluded hermit types who prefer life without WiFi than people.
