Packing

Before you get here, don't forget to pack all the right equipment. You don't want to be arriving at the departure lounge when you suddenly realise you forgot your skinny jeans and your water proof beard wax.

Travel

Finding the right travel option is essential, last year some guys tried to get here using only two things, a peddle boat and hope. Safe to say they were pretty confused when they were greeted by a tribe of pygmy warriors instead of the standard pat on the back welcome we give.

Accomadation

You're probably thinking "You know what, I don't need to pay for accomodation there I can just rough it out for the next week sleeping in tents or in the back of my "totally rad" VW Beetle". No. You will die. Take a look at our lists of possible hosts to accomadate your stay in one of our three different locations.
